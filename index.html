About Raminta

Hi, I’m Raminta — your fitness & nutrition coach

I’m a coach and a mum of three, and I know exactly what it’s like to juggle work, family and everything in between while trying to look after yourself. That’s why my coaching isn’t built on strict diets or punishing routines — it’s built on consistency.

I help busy women rebuild their routine, improve their nutrition, get stronger and feel in control of their body again — with a plan that fits your real life, not someone else’s highlight reel.

Most of my clients work with me online, wherever they are in the world. If you’re local to Welwyn Garden City or Hertfordshire and want hands-on support, you can add in-person personal training sessions too.

How It Works

Your coaching journey

Four simple steps from first chat to lasting results.

1

Consultation

We talk through your goals, struggles, lifestyle and schedule. Together we find where nutrition, meal prep and training fit into your real life — then choose the plan that suits you best.

2

Nutrition Strategy

I calculate your macros, identify your supportive foods and build a balanced routine that feels realistic. No strict diets. No overwhelm. Just structure that works for busy people.

3

Training Program

We set achievable goals based on your lifestyle and gym availability. I create a personalised program that’s effective, simple to follow and designed to get results without adding stress.

4

Accountability

Weekly check-ins to review progress. Daily touchpoints to keep you consistent. Full access to message me anytime. You’re supported every single day — not just once a week.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be local to Welwyn Garden City to work with you?

Not at all. Online coaching is my main service and works wherever you are — the app, check-ins and daily support are all remote. In-person PT is an optional add-on for local clients in Welwyn Garden City and Hertfordshire.

I’ve never followed a nutrition plan before. Is this for beginners?

Yes. Everything is personalised to your starting point. We begin with simple structure — no calorie obsession, no cutting out the foods you love — and build from there.

How much time do I need for training?

Your plan is built around your schedule and gym availability, not the other way round. Most clients train 2–4 times per week with sessions that fit into busy days.

How do I get started?

Choose a plan on Hubfit, or email info@raminta.coach for a free consultation and we’ll find the right fit together.
