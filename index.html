About Raminta

Hi, I’m Raminta — your fitness & nutrition coach

I love working with people who are balancing busy lives — especially those working from home or in a hybrid routine — who have access to a gym and are ready to prioritise themselves again. Most of my clients are between 35–55 and already have a basic understanding of the gym, but want guidance, structure and accountability to take things to the next level.

I help busy men and women rebuild their routine, improve their nutrition, get stronger and feel in control of their body again — with a plan that fits your real life, not someone else’s highlight reel.

My coaching is mainly online, which means you’ll have the flexibility to train on your schedule while still feeling fully supported. You’ll get a personalised workout plan through my app, and I’ll be there to monitor your progress, keep you on track and stay in regular contact so you never feel like you’re doing it alone. I’ll also guide you with your nutrition, helping you build sustainable habits that actually fit your lifestyle.

For those who prefer face-to-face sessions, I offer in-person personal training in Welwyn Garden City too — though spaces are limited and must be booked in advance. I genuinely look forward to supporting you on your journey — helping you feel stronger, more confident, and in control of your training and nutrition every step of the way.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be local to Welwyn Garden City to work with you?

Not at all. Online coaching is my main service and works wherever you are — the app, check-ins and daily support are all remote. In-person PT is an optional add-on for local clients in Welwyn Garden City and Hertfordshire.

I’ve never followed a nutrition plan before. Is this for beginners?

Yes. Everything is personalised to your starting point. We begin with simple structure — no calorie obsession, no cutting out the foods you love — and build from there.

How much time do I need for training?

Your plan is built around your schedule and gym availability, not the other way round. Most clients train 2–4 times per week with sessions that fit into busy days.

How does the training and nutrition work?

After our initial assessment I will work out your fitness and nutrition plans and give you access to an app where I can monitor your progress. All you have to do is log what you eat and train!

How is progress measured?

We’ll use progress photos, weigh-ins and measurements, which are compared in a chart — so you can see the trend, not just a single number.

How do I get started?

Choose a plan on Hubfit, or email info@raminta.coach for a free consultation and we’ll find the right fit together.

Have any more questions?

Feel free to reach out via Instagram or email and I’ll be more than happy to answer anything at all.
